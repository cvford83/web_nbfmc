Our vision is to provide the community with cutting edge ministry that will be a center of worship and development for all races. Our plan is to minister to the whole man through the word of God. To lead the world to worship God through serving, loving, evangelizing, and making disciples. Through the truth and the power of the Holy Spirit, to "Go Ye" and preach and teach the gospel of our Lord and Saviour Jesus Christ. To edify the Kingdom of God by sharing Jesus with others who know of Him, but lack knowing Him as their personal Saviour.

Our mission is to reach the world with the unadulterated Word of God, with signs following; To operate in the five fold gifts, with a message of deliverance according to Ephesians 4:11-13. "And He gave some Apostles; and some Prophets; and and some Evangelists; and some Pastors; and some Teachers for the perfecting of the saints for the edifying of the body of Christ." To reach out to bless the poor, homeless, sick, needy, diseased, oppressed, depressed and those in bondage to all type of works of the enemy. To be a blessing to other ministries in an effort to unify the body of Christ.

Apostle James A. Ponder

James Ponder is the Apostle and Overseer of New Beginning Faith Church in Newnan, GA. His responsibilities are to shepherd, direct, teach, and protect church membership with integrity and humility. He also serves as a leader over the elders in the church. In his capacity, he helps in the governance of church affairs. He has been with the same company, STS Inc., for over 15 years as a field supervisor. STS is a leader in providing Utility Engineering and Damage Prevention Services.

His secular leadership and management experience assists him in bringing efficiencies to the operation of New Beginning Faith Church. James has also served faithfully in various roles at Prayer Warriors in Motion for Christ serving as an usher, a deacon, praise and worship leader, and on the finance committee before joining with his wife to start New Beginning Faith Church, initially as a home Bible study.

It is the vision of Apostle James and Apostle Venus to establish a place of togetherness and unity in the body of Christ. Under his leadership, the church has become a pillar of the community hosting many events such as back to school drives, feeding the homeless, providing clothing and thanksgiving and Christmas dinner baskets to families in need.

In December of 2014 Apostle James and his wife were affirmed as Apostles in the Lord’s church. Apostle Ponder and his wife have been happily married for over 20 years and have two children Javier, and Jamvis; and one grandson, Cayun Maxwell. His family resides in the metro-Atlanta are. When not ministering or volunteering he remains active in sports and spending time with his family. One of the Apostle Ponder’s favorite scriptures is Matthew 18:20 “For where two or three are gathered together in my name, there am I in the midst of them.”

Apostle Venus Ponder

Apostle Venus Ponder is the Apostle and Prophet of New Beginning Faith Church in Newnan, GA. Her responsibilities are to shepherd, direct, teach, and protect church membership with integrity and humility. In her capacity , she stands as the “watchman on the wall,” and shares the prophetic word as it pertains to the body of Christ. In her secular career, she worked in the collegiate arena as a purchasing manager for many years.

She now serves in ministry full time. Venus also served faithfully in various roles at Prayer Warriors in Motion for Christ serving as an usher, deaconess, evangelist, praise and worship leader, and on the finance committee before joining with her husband to start New Beginning Faith Church, initially as a home based Bible study.

As the vision started to take shape, they knew that this would be a ministry of balance, a ministry that focuses on every aspect of the Christian life, ”The Whole You.” In December of 2014 Apostle Venus and her husband were affirmed as Apostles in the Lord’s church. Under their leadership, the church has become a pillar of the community hosting events such as the annual women’s retreat, which has birthed the seed that shares its name, “I Can Do Anything with the Right Pair of Shoes” which Apostle Venus Ponder co-authored with Dr. Carolyn Hall, and the annual Evangelist Betty Bolton Legacy Mother’s Day Luncheon in honor of her late mother.

Apostles James and Venus have been happily married for over 20 years and have two children Javier, and Jamvis; one grandson, Cayun Maxwell. His family resides in the metro-Atlanta area. When not ministering or volunteering they both remain active in sports and spending time with their family. One of Apostle Ponder’s favorite scriptures in Matthew 6:33, “But seek ye first the Kingdom of God and his righteousness; and all these things shall be add unto you.”
